Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike ?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one ?

Inspect your helmet regularly for any visible signs of damage, such as cracks, dents, or deformities. If you see any of these signs, it's time to get a new helmet

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride ?

Before riding a bike that has been in storage, it's important to conduct a thorough inspection to ensure it's safe to ride. Bikes stored for an extended period may experience certain issues that need to be addressed before hitting the road

What rules should I follow when riding my bike ?

When riding your bike, prioritize safety by wearing a helmet and following traffic laws. Obey road rules, ride in the same direction as traffic, and use bike lanes when available. Be visible with lights and reflective gear, and always yield to pedestrians. Stay alert, keep both hands on the handlebars, and check your bike regularly.
